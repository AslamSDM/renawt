Ever wonder what witchcraft happens the second you paste your SaaS product URL into Remawt? It's kinda wild, actually.

First, the "brandAnalyzer" AI agent kicks in. It's like a digital detective, sniffing out your brand colors, fonts, and overall vibe. Then the "scriptWriter" starts crafting a demo script, pulling the key features and benefits from your site.

Next, our "creativeDirector" imagines how those features will POP visually. Should there be glassmorphism? Scale-bounce? A sick aurora gradient? Yes, yes, and probably yes. (Unless you're super corporate, in which case, Editorial Clean it is).

Finally, the "codeGenerator" translates that creative vision into React code using Remotion. Think of it as building a mini video studio, scene by scene. Boom. Your video is rendering.

All in under 5 mins.
