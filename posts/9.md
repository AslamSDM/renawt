Okay, Remotion. Let's talk about it.

We looked at *everything* for Remawt. Seriously. Adobe After Effects scripting? Been there, tried that. (Headache doesn't even begin to cover it.) Straight-up ffmpeg? My *god* no.

So why Remotion?

Two reasons, really. First, React. We're a Next.js shop. Frontend is in our blood. The idea of building video with components felt...right. Like finally doing video the *sane* way.

But second – and this is the sneaky one – it unlocked creativity. Sounds corny, I know. But not wrestling with the tool actually lets you *think* about what you're making. And THAT is worth its weight in gold. Plus, beat-synced animations are just way too fun. Admit it.
