Okay, let's talk real quick about something I've been chewing on. Demo videos. Seems simple, right?

It's easy to look at Remawt's pricing and go, "Hmm, do I *really* need this?"

But have you actually done the math on what *not* having a decent product demo video costs you?

Think about it. Say your sales team spends an average of 30 minutes explaining the basics of your SaaS product on every intro call. And let's say they do 10 of those calls a week. That's 5 hours, just *explaining* things.

Salary + benefits... that's gotta be at least $50/hour fully loaded, right? (Probably way more, depending on your team). So that's $250 a week. $1000 a month, just on *basic explanations*.

A good demo video can cut that explanation time in half. At least.

So, are you really saving money by skipping the demo video? Or are you bleeding it slowly?

Just food for thought.
