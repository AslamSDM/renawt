Remember when making a freemium SaaS video meant stringing together iMovie clips at 2 AM?

Yeah. Me too. I basically *lived* on gas station coffee and ramen for six months back in 2022.

I hated everything about that process. The clunkiness, the endless export times, the feeling I had no idea what I was doing.

I swore then, sleep-deprived and jittery, that there HAD to be a better way. That somewhere, somehow, the pain could be automated away. Turns out, "somewhere, somehow" was inside my own dumb head.

Now it's Remawt. And I haven't touched iMovie since.
