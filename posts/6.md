The biggest facepalm moment building Remawt?

Assuming "AI video" was enough of a hook.

Turns out, "AI" is just table stakes now. Everyone's got some AI angle. Doesn't mean it's *good* AI. Doesn't mean it solves a real problem.

We spent weeks optimizing our AI scriptWriter agent (powered by Gemini, btw, because speed matters) and nobody cared until we started highlighting *what* the AI was automating — specifically, the soul-crushing process of scripting product demos.

The lesson? Features tell, benefits sell. Even in the age of Skynet.
